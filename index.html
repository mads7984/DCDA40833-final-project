Prescription Drug Price Comparison

Comparing TrumpRx, GoodRx, SingleCare, and Cost Plus Drugs for Type 2 Diabetes Medications

Welcome

Welcome to my final project website. This site presents a comparison of prescription drug prices, drug availability, and pricing transparency across TrumpRx, GoodRx, SingleCare, and Cost Plus Drugs.

The purpose of this website is to present the findings in a user friendly and organized format. Each page focuses on a different part of the analysis so the data is easier to interpret and compare.

Project Overview

Prescription drug prices can be difficult to compare because websites present price information in different ways. Some platforms emphasize coupon prices, some display starting prices, and some provide clearer information than others about restrictions, dosage, quantity, and eligibility.

To make these differences easier to understand, this project focuses on a selected group of Type 2 diabetes medications and compares how each platform communicates pricing and access information.

In addition to price, I also evaluated transparency by looking at whether the site clearly displayed dosage, quantity, price type, restrictions, pharmacy variation, and ease of access.
